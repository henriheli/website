Murre

Exploring healthcare and compliance at sea.

About

Murre studies how healthcare and technology intersect in the maritime sector. The current focus is on how shipping and offshore organizations manage medical compliance - from crew health certifications to onboard medical processes.

We speak with professionals across operations, healthcare, and regulation to understand what’s working, where the challenges are, and how practices could evolve.

Purpose

Maritime healthcare compliance is essential but complex. Regulations differ, systems don’t always align, and those responsible for compliance manage competing priorities. Murre aims to build a clearer picture of this space and share insights that help improve health, safety, and efficiency across the industry.
